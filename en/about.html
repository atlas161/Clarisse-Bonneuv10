Clarisse Bonneu model profile

This page presents Clarisse Bonneu's visual universe, background and approach to fashion, beauty and editorial projects.

Clarisse Bonneu develops a visual universe rooted in fashion, beauty and editorial imagery. Through this portfolio, she aims to show a natural on-camera presence, a clear silhouette and a genuine ability to adapt to different artistic directions.

This profile is intended for model agencies, brands, photo studios, digital productions and creative teams looking for a model able to support campaigns, lookbooks, social media content, e-commerce shoots and portrait sessions.

Each collaboration is approached with close attention to shooting rhythm, posture consistency, visual precision and a smooth working process on set as well as in studio environments.

Key strengths

  • Available for editorial shoots, beauty projects, campaigns, lookbooks and brand content.
  • Well suited to the needs of agencies, castings, art direction teams and visual productions.
  • A portfolio designed to allow a quick reading of series, categories and visual references.
